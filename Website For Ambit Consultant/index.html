Who Are We?

Founded on a shared vision of revolutionizing the energy sector, Ambit Energy began its journey in 2006 when founders Jere Thompson, Jr. and Chris Chambless saw an opportunity in energy deregulation. Starting with humble beginnings—a simple lunch conversation—they set out to create what would become one of America's most esteemed retail energy providers. Based in Dallas, Texas, Ambit Energy is committed to offering affordable electricity and gas without compromising integrity for growth.

Ambit Energy quickly gained momentum, achieving significant milestones such as surpassing nearly $200 million in revenue by 2008 and nearly $325 million by 2009. Recognized as the fastest-growing privately-held company in North Texas, Ambit's exceptional growth continued, culminating in being named "America's Fastest-Growing Private Company" by Inc. magazine in 2010. These achievements reflect Ambit's dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction, further underscored by being ranked among the Top 100 Places to Work by the Dallas Morning News.

Ambit Energy's commitment to unparalleled customer care is supported by the innovative BlueNet™ system, ensuring efficient account management and superior service. Today, Ambit Energy stands as one of the fastest-growing retail energy providers nationwide, energized by a bright future ahead for its team, consultants, and valued customers.

What do we do?

Ambit Energy operates nationwide, offering electricity and natural gas services in deregulated markets across the United States. Leveraging a direct sales model through a network of over 600,000 Independent Consultants, Ambit provides consumers with cost-effective energy options. Headquartered in Dallas, Texas, Ambit Energy aims to establish itself as the premier retail energy provider in America, committed to delivering quality service and innovative solutions. Recognized as the #1 Fastest-Growing Private Company in America by Inc. magazine in 2010, Ambit continues to lead as the fastest-growing company in the retail energy sector today.
